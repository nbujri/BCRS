Frequently Asked Questions

Need an answer to something? Find it here!
User Configuration How do I add, edit, and delete a user? Step 1: After a successful log-in, navigate to the 'User Config' link on the navigation bar. On the right side of the page, there will be a form titled 'Add User'.
Step 2: Enter the required information for the user that you want to add.
Step 3: Press the submit button and the user will be added to the list.
Step 4: To delete a user, click the trash can under the 'Actions' tab, and to edit a user's information, click the pen that is also under the 'Actions' tab.
Account Registration How do I register for an account?

Step 1: At the sign-in page, there will be a button labeled 'Register', click on it and it will take you to the 'User Registration' page.
Step 2: Once at the 'User Registration' page, fill out the 'Account Credentials', 'Personal Information', and select your Security Questions. Once those are filled out, click 'register'.
Step 3: You'll then be taken back to the sign-in page. Use the email and the password you chose to sign in.
Step 4: Seeing the home page means a successful sign-in!

Password Reset How do I reset my password?

Step 1: At the sign-in page, there will be a button labeled 'Forgot Password', click on it and it will redirect you to the 'Forgot Password' page.
Step 2: Once you are redirected, enter your email address. Once your email address is entered, hit submit.
Step 3: You will then be redirected to the security questions you answered when you registered for an account. Enter those answers and hit verify.
Step 4: Once the security questions are verified, you will be prompted to change your password.

Service Repair Request How do I submit a service repair request?

Step 1: On the navigation bar, click on the link that says 'Service Repairs'
Step 2: There will be be a button marked 'add a new invoice', click on the link and it will redirect you to another page.
Step 3: Now that you are at the Customer Invoice page, fill out the form with the necessary information. Click the submit invoice button.
Step 4: Once submitted you will be taken back to the Service Repairs page where the new invoice you made is there.
